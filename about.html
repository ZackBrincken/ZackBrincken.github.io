About Me
Why Computer Science?
Throughout my early life and into high school, I was uncertain of the career path I wanted to follow. Of course, I had played computer games all my life, but that is no reason alone to pursue programming. However, I would largely attribute my passion (or at least my discovery of it) to Minecraft. When I was younger, I was obsessed with writing command block instructions to make things happen in unmodded Minecraft which would have been otherwise impossible. While it is not exactly "programming" in the traditional sense, it requires a moderately in-depth knowledge of game mechanics and the scripting language which command blocks use to function. Ultimately, I think "programming" these command blocks in addition to my love for problem-solving led me to computer science and I could not be more thankful.
