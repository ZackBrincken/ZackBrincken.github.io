FamousGuitars
FamousGuitars is a database driven website I designed for a final project in my CS234 Databases and Web Development course throughout Fall 2024 at SIUE. While this was, at it's core, a school project, I really enjoyed the process and learned some valuable lessons. For one, front-end web development is great, but I am really a back-end fan. Also, this project really taught me the dangers of scope creep as the deadline approached. The last month of this project was a real grind and I learned to keep the scope of my endeavors under control from then on.
